What is Wornout Fashion?

Wornout recyclable fashion is the practice of taking garments at the end of their conventional lifecycle — faded, frayed, stretched — and transforming them into something new. It's not charity shopping. It's a manufacturing philosophy.

From shredded denim becoming insulation to bleached tees repurposed as industrial wipes, the wornout garment is a raw material, not a reject.
